Website Pages and Their Purpose

  • Storyboard Page

    Intent: Provide a visual layout plan and design for the entire website.

    Purpose: Showcase the conceptual wireframes, layout design, color themes, and features planned for each webpage.

  • Index/Home Page

    Intent: Serve as the introductory page for the website.

    Purpose: Welcome visitors with an overview of the site, introduce yourself, and provide easy navigation to other sections through a navigation bar.

  • Personality Page

    Intent: Highlight personal traits and interests.

    Purpose: Showcase your strongest personality traits, hobbies, and how they impact your academic life. Include a favorite quote or motto, information about your hero, and results from a personality assessment with a reflection.

  • Personal Development Page

    Intent: Share insights and lessons learned for personal growth.

    Purpose: Provide reflections on Twelve Pillars chapters and explain how they can be applied to your life. Highlight your educational background and the top two skills you've developed so far.

  • Professional Page

    Intent: Present your professional aspirations and qualifications.

    Purpose: Include a modern-designed resume and CV, along with your vision and mission statements. Detail the career path, job requirements, and qualifications for your future desired role.

  • Reference Page

    Intent: Provide bibliographic sources of information used for the website.

    Purpose: List books, websites, and other resources in APA format that contributed to the content of the website.
